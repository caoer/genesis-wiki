# effects/

Every deployed artifact gets one descriptor page here carrying a pin to its origin + checksum; every change bumps the pin; the audit tier verifies pins resolve and checksums reproduce (contract C37 `effect-pin-lifecycle`). Empty at birth — pages appear with the first deploy.
